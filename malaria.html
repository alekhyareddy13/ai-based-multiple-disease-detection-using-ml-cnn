Early Detection for Malaria

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites transmitted through mosquito bites. Malaria can be a deadly disease if not diagnosed and treated quickly. Several theories like Miasma theory, Water Contamination theory, etc., have been proposed by popular scientists stating the intense impact of this disease on human well-being. It is transmitted through the bite of an infected female Anopheles mosquito, which injects Plasmodium parasites into the bloodstream, causing the disease. Early detection and treatment can prevent severe complications.
