Early Detection for Better Health

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women. Breast cancer is a disease where abnormal cells within the breast tissue begin to grow and multiply uncontrollably, forming tumors that can spread to other parts of the body if left untreated; it most commonly originates in the milk ducts or lobules of the breast, and while primarily affecting women, men can also develop it, although rarely; symptoms may include a breast lump, skin changes like dimpling or puckering, nipple discharge, or changes in breast size or shape, and early detection through screening is crucial for successful treatment. Early detection through AI-based screening can significantly improve treatment success rates and survival.
