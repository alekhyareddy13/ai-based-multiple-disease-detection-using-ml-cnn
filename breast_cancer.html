Early Detection for Better Health

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers among women. It is a disease where abnormal cells within the breast tissue begin to grow and multiply uncontrollably, forming tumors that can spread to other parts of the body if left untreated. Symptoms may include a breast lump, skin changes like dimpling or puckering, nipple discharge, or changes in breast size or shape. Early detection through AI-based screening can significantly improve treatment success rates and survival.
