Early Detection for Better Liver

Liver disease refers to a range of conditions that impair the liver's ability to function properly, often caused by factors like excessive alcohol consumption, viral infections (hepatitis), or fatty buildup, leading to progressive damage and scarring (cirrhosis) within the organ; symptoms may include jaundice (yellowing of skin and eyes), fatigue, abdominal pain, and swelling, while early stages might not show any noticeable signs; early diagnosis and treatment are crucial to manage liver disease and prevent complications like liver failure or cancer.
