Early Detection for Kidney Disease

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a progressive condition where the kidneys lose their function over time. Early detection is crucial as CKD can lead to kidney failure, requiring dialysis or a kidney transplant. Symptoms may include fatigue, swelling, changes in urine output, and high blood pressure. Detecting kidney disease early through medical tests can help manage symptoms and slow progression.
