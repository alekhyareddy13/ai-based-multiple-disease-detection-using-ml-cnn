Early Detection for Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. It develops gradually and can start with barely noticeable tremors in just one hand. The disease is also characterized by stiffness and slowing of movement. Over time, individuals may experience difficulties with balance, speech, and automatic movements. Early detection is crucial for managing symptoms effectively and improving quality of life. Advances in AI-based screening can help in identifying Parkinson's disease at its early stages, allowing for timely medical intervention.
