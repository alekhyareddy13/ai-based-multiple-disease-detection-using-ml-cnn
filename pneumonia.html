Early Detection for Better Lung

Pneumonia is a severe lung infection that occurs when the immune system attacks an infection in the lung's air sacs. This infection causes the air sacs to swell and fill with fluid or pus,making it difficult to breathe. Pneumonia can be effected due to some bacteria like Pneumococci etc. It can be life-threatening if not detected early.
